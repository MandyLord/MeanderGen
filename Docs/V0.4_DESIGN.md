# MeanderGen v0.4 – Direction Planning

## Goal

Replace reactive steering with intelligent path planning.

Instead of choosing a random direction and correcting it afterwards,
the planner will evaluate multiple candidate directions before each step
and choose the highest scoring option.

---

## Candidate Generation

For each movement step:

- Generate a range of candidate turn angles.
- Each candidate represents one possible next direction.
- All candidates are scored independently.

Example:

- -45°
- -40°
- -35°
...
- 0°
...
35°
40°
45°

---

## Scoring

Each candidate receives a score based on:

- Distance from existing path.
- Distance from boundaries.
- Smoothness of movement.
- Preference for open space.
- Future expansion potential.

The highest scoring candidate is selected.

---

## Design Principles

The planner should:

- Never deliberately self-intersect.
- Prefer smooth curves.
- Fill empty space evenly.
- Avoid creating dead ends.
- Produce attractive embroidery paths.

---

## Future Extensions

Possible additional scoring:

- Motif placement.
- Density balancing.
- Regional filling.
- Spiral generation.
- Decorative path styles.
